turtlebot3
配置机器人型号 export TURTLEBOT3_MODEL=waffle_pi
启动gazebo仿真 roslaunch turtlebot3_gazebo turtlebot3_house.launch
启动gmapping SLAM roslaunch turtlebot3_slam turtlebot3_slam.launch slam_methods:=gmapping (or cartographer, hector, karto, frontier_exploration)

启动导航movebase roslaunch turtlebot3_navigation move_base.launch
启动explore-lite（订阅movebase） roslaunch explore-lite explore.launch


move_base 指定参数增加命名空间
